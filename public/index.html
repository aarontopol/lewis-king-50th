Celebrating Lewis King

From Murphy, North Carolina to the global stage — a life defined by dedication, expertise, and great taste in cocktails

Born and raised in Murphy, North Carolina, Lewis King grew up with his brother Stephen in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains before heading east to attend the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill — a proud Tar Heel through and through. Ever the adventurer, Lewis spent a semester studying abroad in Madrid, where he picked up Spanish (yes, he's fluent!) and no doubt developed his refined palate for international cuisine and cocktails.

Lewis launched his EY career in Atlanta before crossing the Atlantic to London, then returning stateside to Charlotte and on to New York — where he earned his partnership. His expertise in international tax has guided major multinational clients through the complexities of cross-border taxation and global compliance. Along the way, he was even designated The Most Patient Man in New York in 2024 — a title that anyone who has worked with Lewis would say is well deserved.

Beyond the technical brilliance, those who work alongside Lewis know him for his legendary wit, his warmth, and his genuine care for the people around him. A proud father to his son Will, a self-described foodie, fluent in Spanish and now learning Italian and French, and a connoisseur of a well-crafted cocktail, Lewis brings the same passion to life outside the office as he does inside it. Here's to 50 years of building a better working world — and enjoying every bite and sip along the way.

The Lewis King Journey

From the Blue Ridge Mountains to the boardrooms of the world

Murphy, NC
Where It All Began
Lewis grew up in Murphy, NC alongside his brother Stephen — a small mountain town that instilled the values of hard work, community, and good Southern hospitality that would define his career.
Chapel Hill & Madrid
Tar Heel & World Traveler
Headed to UNC Chapel Hill, where he became a lifelong Tar Heel. Spent a semester abroad in Madrid — picking up fluent Spanish and a taste for international adventure that would shape his entire career.
Atlanta
EY — The Beginning
Joined Ernst & Young in Atlanta, quickly establishing himself as a rising talent in the tax practice and beginning a journey that would span continents.
London • 2005–2007
Crossing the Atlantic
Took his talents to EY's London office, gaining invaluable global perspective and deepening his international tax advisory capabilities on the world stage.
Charlotte • 2007–2009
Back Stateside
Returned to the U.S. and joined EY's Charlotte office, building deep client relationships and expanding his advisory reach across the Southeast.
New York • 2009–2016
The Big Apple & Partnership
Brought his global expertise to NYC, navigating some of the most complex international tax engagements in the firm — and earned his promotion to International Tax Partner. Officially named The Most Patient Man in New York in 2024.
Today • Atlanta
Home & Turning 50
Back home in Atlanta, Lewis continues to lead as an International Tax Partner while being a proud father to his son Will. When he's not navigating global tax codes, you'll find him exploring the city's food scene, learning Italian and French, perfecting his cocktail of choice, or deploying that famous wit.
